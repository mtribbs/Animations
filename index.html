Sleeping Routines

How many hours per day you should sleep varies, but at a minimum you should get at least 13 hours of nap time. Preferably you should sleep about 15-16 hours. Space out your naps throughout the day. It does not matter when you sleep, but you should definitely try to get up at five in the morning and run around and make noise when the humans are sleeping.

Being awake for too long at a time is exhausting, try to even out your naps.

If you have a housemate, try to adjust to each others' sleeping routines.
